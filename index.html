The Johnny Coppola Story

Johnny Coppola is a torchbearer of the classic American songbook tradition, bringing the sophistication and charm of the Rat Pack era to contemporary audiences.

With a voice that channels the golden age of jazz and swing, Johnny's performances are a masterclass in timeless musicality. His repertoire includes both beloved standards and original compositions that feel instantly classic.

From intimate jazz clubs to the legendary Studio A at Capitol Records, Johnny has captivated audiences with his smooth vocals, impeccable phrasing, and undeniable stage presence.

When not performing, Johnny can be found mentoring young musicians, preserving the legacy of America's musical heritage for future generations.
